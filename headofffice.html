HEADOFRFICE™

Be interesting on the internet

Being simply ‘good’ is no longer ‘good enough’ for today's audiences. Being a successful digital brand takes much more. Your time-poor and emotionally distracted audience craves a distinctiveness that a traditional agency can no longer provide.

Stop producing digital content to satisfy algorithms, to win awards or to fill a slot in a content plan, and start making content that makes your audience think and feel.

We understand that your branded digital content needs to perform a variety of complex tasks, but engaging an audience should be at the heart of everything. You need to partner with a team that understands each element, and can pull it all together to create a cohesive, immersive story.

About Us

HeadOfffice™ is a small, multi-skilled team of designers, developers and writers, working in partnership with brands, publishers and audiences. We focus on developing our own inhouse digital publications as well as selling our creative services to a select group of brand partners.

We believe that the traditional trappings of large creative agencies - the bureaucracy and politics, the hierarchy and job titles - are detrimental to the creative process. An organisation focused on justifying the existence of its own bloated structure is fundamentally at odds with the production of interesting, creative and ground-breaking work.

We have freed ourselves from this archaic way of working to create a modern, connected studio that brings our varied skill sets to the fore, truly collaborating to create digital content that is new and diffferent.

We pride ourselves on delivering a more authentic and transparent way of working with our clients. We promote a heuristic culture within the HeadOfffice™ studio. Where all team members regardless of their specific role must present an aptitude for creativity, the use of technology and marketing methods. We aren’t siloed, separate or self interested, there’s no ‘us and them’; we are one team, working together to produce creative, considered, audience-first content that works for our partners.

Founded by Award-Winning Creative Director Gaz Battersby

HeadOfffice™ was founded by Gaz Battersby in 2016. Over his 15-year career in the creative and marketing sector, Gaz has delivered large-scale digital creative campaigns at the highest level, while also building and managing large multi-skilled creative and technical teams. Gaz has a reputation for generating great ideas and knowing how to make them work, helping brands to grow.

Jaywing plc adds Social Audiences platform with acquisition of HeadOfffice

Jaywing has announced today the acquisition of social publishing, native marketing and audience first creative agency HeadOfffice. The agency will become part of its Leeds based digital marketing business Epiphany.

HeadOfffice was founded in 2016 by former Epiphany Creative Director, Gaz Battersby and has grown quickly since.

Tom Salmon, MD of Epiphany, commented: "It’s really great to have Gaz back in the fold, both in terms of the top drawer creative skills he and his team will bring to Epiphany and the emerging opportunities in social audience creation and engagement. As Creative Partner, Gaz will oversee all of Epiphany’s creative output and the development of new creative propositions, as well as the further development of HeadOfffice. The addition of the HeadOfffice team to our studio means we have bolstered our creative strength to meet growing demand from a broad range of clients, as well as adding valuable and unique social publishing and amplification experience. All of this makes HeadOfffice a great fit with Epiphany."

Commenting on the deal, Rob Shaw CEO of Jaywing said: "I’ve known Gaz for many years and I’m really impressed with what he has created in HeadOfffice. We know that the worlds of brand and performance marketing are coming together, with Google and Facebook leading many of those changes that favour brands that connect with their customers. Putting HeadOfffice’s creative excellence and social content alongside what we already excel in across Epiphany means that we’re perfectly positioned to let our clients take advantage of this change."

"I’m absolutely thrilled to be back working with Rob and Tom", said Battersby. "Having taken the HeadOfffice proposition from a one man start-up to an established team with retained clients and a growing social audience platform, the time felt right to take it to the next level with the opportunities being part of a bigger business brings. My time at Epiphany was one of the most rewarding periods of my career and I’m quite sure it will be again now, with its renewed vigour and focus on its creative direction. The fact that we’ve all worked together and know each other well means that we’re going to be able to deliver great work for clients from day one."
